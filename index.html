Living cells:

simulate generation(s)

ms between generations

Drag&Drop Patterns




Rules
  1. Alive cell dies if has fewer than two live neighbours (under-population).

  2. Alive cell dies if has more than three live neighbours (overcrowding).

  3. Alive cell lives on to the next generation if has two or three live neighbours.

  4. Dead cell with exactly three live neighbours becomes a live cell (reproduction).
